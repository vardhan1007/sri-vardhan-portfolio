Character Profiles

Kenji // 健二

Protagonist

A quiet high school student who possesses the rare ability to see spiritual connections between objects and memories. Wields ancient ink scroll techniques.

Hiro // 大輝

Rival / Comrade

A skilled martial artist with an intense determination to prove himself. Specializes in energy manipulation and fights alongside Kenji to maintain balance.

Sora // 空

Mentor

An enigmatic wanderer who guides Kenji in utilizing his ink powers. Holds keys to the connection matrix and coordinates the rebellion.
